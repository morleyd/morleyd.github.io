How to Use

Welcome to my Wordle Helper tool!

Instructions: Simply drag the letters as you see them onto the wordle board.

  • Toggle the "Correct" or green color for letters you know are correctly placed
  • Toggle the "Wrong Spot" or yellow color for letters contained in the word somewhere
  • Use the "Illegal Letters" text box for any letters that you know aren't in the word

I hope this helps with the original Wordle created by Josh Wardle.
